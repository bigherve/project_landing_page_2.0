My 4 favourite car models

The Toyota Corolla is one of the most popular cars of all time worldwide. It was introduced in 1966 and in 1974, it became the best-selling car worldwide. This model currently brings in six figures for Toyota every year. The Corolla gets good milage, has good reliability, and is cheap to fix. What more can you ask for in a entry level car.

In 1975 VW added the GTI badge to the golf creating the first hot hatch and has forever been the words benchmark for hot hatches. The GTI has to be one of the most practical sports cars around, it's fast for most, it's stylish, it has a premium feel, it's reliable and the resent models have all the gadgets. The list goes on I mean come on it ticks all the boxes it's the perfect all rounder.

The Civic is one of hondas top selling models every year since it was introduced in 1972. Your average Joe on the street knows the Honda civic by name witch says a lot. The Civic also has great resale value because of it's bulletproof engines. Civics are very safe with a 5-star safety rating from NHTSA and multiple Top Safety Pick awards from IIHS, civics has proven to be one of the safest cars around. Plus there earlier sporty engine options used to rev to the high heavens with a very nice sound and I will forever love them for that.
